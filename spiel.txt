The Continuous Improvement & Projects (CIP) team is responsible for the execution of  established Projects for the Asia region by applying project management and continuous improvement discipline in line with our Citi standards and methodologies. The team consists of Project Managers and Automations Engineers which drives operational efficiency and promotes a continuous improvement culture through various programs including LEAN, Bright Ideas, Innovation Café, and automation tools such as Robotics Process Automation (RPA).  The Project Managers implements Location Strategy projects to deliver on continuous effort in standardizing products and processes in our strategic centers and across the broader region. They also lead Migrations initiatives to enable work transfer from HR/Businesses into HRSS. Likewise, the Automation Engineers are tasked to provide oversight and planning for technology related work across programs/projects. They are also responsible in delivering automation / technology requirements for the project which results into productivity saves.
